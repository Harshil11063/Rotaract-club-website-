About Our Club

Who We Are

We are a dynamic group of young leaders and community enthusiasts dedicated to making a positive difference. As a Rotaract club, we are part of a global community that brings together 18-30 year olds to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for young people to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.

Our Vision

To cultivate a vibrant and inclusive community of engaged young professionals and students, fostering leadership, ethics, and service above self. We aim to be a beacon of positive change, inspiring action and creating sustainable solutions for local and global challenges.

Our Club's Journey

2021 - Founding Stone

The Rotaract Club of NLJIET was chartered in 2021 with a vision to bring together like-minded young individuals passionate about service and fellowship. Our journey began with a small group of enthusiastic students.

2022 - First Major Projects

In 2022, we successfully executed our first significant community projects, focusing on education and environmental awareness. These projects set the tone for our commitment to impactful service.

2023 - Expansion & Collaboration

Our club saw significant growth in 2023, expanding our membership and establishing key partnerships with local NGOs and other Rotaract clubs. We hosted several inter-club events, increasing our reach and influence.

Present - Continuing the Legacy

Today, we continue to uphold the Rotaract values, engaging in diverse projects, fostering leadership, and building strong friendships. We look forward to many more years of service and fellowship in Ahmedabad and beyond!

Join Our Family!

Are you a young, enthusiastic individual between 18 and 30 years old, passionate about making a difference and building lifelong friendships? Then Rotaract is the place for you!

Why Join Rotaract?

  • Service Opportunities: Engage in meaningful community service projects.
  • Leadership Development: Hone your leadership, public speaking, and organizational skills.
  • Professional Networking: Connect with Rotarians and professionals from various fields.
  • Fellowship & Fun: Build lasting friendships and participate in exciting social events.
  • Global Connection: Be part of a worldwide network of over 200,000 Rotaractors.
